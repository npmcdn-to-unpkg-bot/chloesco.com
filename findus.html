How to find Chloé...

Chloé's French Café is located inside the Landmark Executive Center, north-west of the town of Santa Rosa.

The café is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 am-5:00 pm.
Closed Saturday • Sunday

We are, easy to find....

    Coming from Healdsburg: take 101-south to the Mendocino Avenue exit, turn right at the first stop light (Hopper Avenue), then right again on Airway Drive. The Landmark Executive Center is the brick office building, located at the end of Airway Drive.

    Coming from downtown Santa Rosa: take 101-north to the Mendocino Avenue exit, turn right on old Redwood Hwy, and right again to cross over the highway. At the light, turn right on Cleveland (which turns into Hopper Avenue), and at the second light, turn right on Airway Drive. The Landmark Executive Center is the brick office building, located at the end of Airway Drive.

To find us, just go to your left, once you have been through the main entrance door. We look forward to seeing you there!
